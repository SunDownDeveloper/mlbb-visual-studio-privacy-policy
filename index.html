Privacy Policy for MLBB Visual Studio

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MLBB Visual Studio ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. By installing or using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Process

1.1 Local Game Data (Processed Only)

MLBB Visual Studio operates as a local file modification tool. To function, the app requires Storage Access Framework (SAF) or Shizuku (Root-Level) permissions to inject visual files directly into your local game directory.

This information is processed STRICTLY on your device. It is not collected, stored, uploaded, or shared by us in any way. We do not have access to your game account, passwords, or personal gameplay data.

1.2 Error Logs and App Usage Reports (Optional)

We may collect crash logs, error reports, or app usage information only if you choose to manually send the report. This may include:

  • Crash details and stack traces
  • App performance logs and injection engine status
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android OS version

This data contains absolutely no personally identifiable information. It is used exclusively by our development team to identify bugs and improve the app's stability across different Android versions.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

MLBB Visual Studio uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to support ongoing development. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interactions
  • Approximate location (for regional ad targeting)

Google's data practices are described in Google's Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy.

You can disable personalized ads at any time via your device's Google Ad Settings.

3. How We Use Collected Data

We use optional crash and usage data exclusively for:

  • Fixing bugs and resolving engine injection crashes
  • Improving app performance and UI responsiveness
  • Enhancing overall user experience

We do not sell your data, share it with any third party (other than Google AdMob for serving ads), or track you outside the app.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any optional diagnostic data you send. However, please be aware that no method of electronic data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Children's Privacy

MLBB Visual Studio is not specifically targeted at children, but it may be used by all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Control & Choices

  • Revoke Access: You may revoke Storage or Shizuku folder access at any time via your device settings or the app's Control Center.
  • Diagnostic Data: You may choose not to send crash logs when prompted.
  • Ads: You may disable personalized ads through your device's Google settings.
  • Total Opt-Out: You may uninstall the app at any time to instantly cease all local data processing.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect new features or Android OS requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
